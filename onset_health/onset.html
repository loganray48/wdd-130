Who We Are

Onset Health was built on making people healthier and happier through fitness. We know gyms can be intimidating, but when you walk through our doors, you won't find any one stereotype here. We are the gym where everyone belongs. We go out of our way to provide a supportive environment where people can try new things, meet new people, and build a community.
